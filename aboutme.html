How it all started!

It began when I was assigned my Social Justice Culminating to find an issue and to actually do an action to help that issue out. I was told to do something that aligns to my interests which was mainly coding in terms of creating stuff, so I decided to make a website. I was led down a rabbit hole on how making a website can help an issue, when I realized from another teacher of mine's story that there's a lot of scholarships for minorities to help them out but because people don't look around they don't find it. I realized by making a website that contains a list of scholarships for minorities, I would be able to help people who need the funding to be able to look for a scholarship without taking too much of their time. That led to what you see here today!
